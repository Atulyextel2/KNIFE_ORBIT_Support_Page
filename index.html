About Knife Orbit

A quick overview of what the game is and how it plays.

Core Gameplay

In Knife Orbit, knives rotate around a central fruit core. You tap the screen to throw new knives at the core while avoiding collisions with blades already stuck there. Timing, spacing, and pattern recognition are key.

Key Features

  • Fast-paced one-tap arcade gameplay.
  • Orbit patterns that become faster and more challenging as you progress.
  • Short, replayable runs focused on beating your high score.
  • Clean hyper-casual visuals and responsive feel.

Note: Some visual or gameplay details may change across updates as the game is improved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Knife Orbit.

How do I play Knife Orbit?

Tap anywhere on the screen to throw a knife toward the center. Your goal is to stick all required knives into the fruit core without hitting any existing knives. If a new knife hits an existing one, the run ends.

The game feels very fast. Is there a difficulty setting?

Knife Orbit is designed as a quick-reaction arcade game. Difficulty increases gradually based on your progress. There is no manual difficulty slider at launch, but balance and pacing may be adjusted in updates based on feedback.

Will I lose progress if I reinstall the game?

Progress handling may depend on platform and store configuration. If you are worried about losing local progress, we recommend keeping the app installed until you no longer need your current scores or unlocks.

Is Knife Orbit online or offline?

Knife Orbit is primarily designed for quick offline play sessions. Some builds or future updates may use network connectivity for analytics, updates, or additional features, but core gameplay does not require you to be online.

Troubleshooting

Before contacting support, please try these checks if you face issues.

Game Not Opening or Crashing

  • Restart your device and try opening the game again.
  • Make sure your OS is updated to the latest stable version.
  • Update Knife Orbit to the latest version available in your app store.
  • If the issue persists, reinstall the game.
  • If crashes continue, capture a screenshot or screen recording and send it to support.

Input Lag or Performance Issues

  • Close heavy apps running in the background.
  • Check that your device has enough free storage.
  • Disable battery-saver modes that throttle performance.

Progress or Score Not Updating

  • Finish the current run and ensure the summary screen appears.
  • Avoid force-closing the app right after a stage ends.

Privacy & Data

How Knife Orbit handles your data.

Data We Store

Knife Orbit stores gameplay-related data such as scores, progress, and basic settings needed to run the game and provide a good experience.

Device Permissions

Depending on your platform, Knife Orbit may request basic device permissions needed to save data or access the network for updates and analytics. These are used only to support the game’s operation.
